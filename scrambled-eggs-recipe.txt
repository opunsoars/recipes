Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 large onion
 - 1 green chilli
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teasponn pepper
 - 1/2 teaspoon bishop's weed
 - 1 tablespoon refined oil

Directions:
 1. Preheat frying pan with refined oil.
 
 2. Chop the onion into tiny lot and mix with chopped green chilli.
 
 3. saute the onion and green chilli along with bishop's weed in the oil at a low flame.

 4. While this is on, beat the 2 eggs and add salt and pepper to the mix.

 5. When the onion starts to turn golden brown, pour the egg batter.

 6. Let the eggspread cook for about 30 seconds on high flame. Reduce flame to simmer 
    and start scrambling the mix.

7. Once no more coagulated eggs left, switch the gas off and let the scrambled eggs 
    stay in the pan. Ready to eat after a minute.
